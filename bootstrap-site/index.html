About Frog Con

Frog con is the ultimate destination for frog lovers and hobbyists alike. Located in Kyle's room, this con will be one to tell your grandchildren about for sure. Frog con is the place to learn from the experts and connect with other hobbyists. With over 300 vendors, it will be hard to leave empty handed! Don't miss out on the excitement!!!

Expert Speakers

I have paid experts lots of money to come to this con so please buy a ticket so that I can begin to dig myself out of debt! These experts have been breeding frogs for decades and have so much knowledge to share with you.

If you consider yourself a real frogger man or woman, you will not want to miss out on these speaker keynotes. You will be a fool if you do! They have the secrets to success in the dart frog hobby.

These speakers are coming from all corners of the globe to attend this con. Frog-Con is truly about bringing froggers of all nationalities together in celebration of our lil froggy friends. Come join the love!

What You'll Learn

Dart Frog Basics: Learn the basics of dart frog care here. Beginners welcome! Species Overview: A comprehensive look at the main species in the hobby. Dart frog husbandry: Learn tips and tricks from expert breeders! Vivariums 101: Learn how to make a beautiful and bioactive home for your froggy friends! Insects and Feeders: Get the scoop on the best feeders and clean up crew bugs.

Speakers

Will "The Frog Man" Johnson

Hailing from Venezuela, Will is truly an expert of the hobby. When he was a little boy, his dad would take him out to the rainforest and they would spend hours upon hours spotting colorful frogs. Will is a husbandry specialist and has a wealth of valuable information to share.

Favorite Frog: Dendrobates Tinctorius "Azureus"

Jason Frogs

The origins of Jason Frogs are quite ominous. He appeared on the forums one day with a massive collection of the rarest frogs. He is believed to be about 109 years old and comes from Costa Rica. He is coming to Frog Con to share some of his ancient secrets.

Favorite Frog: O. Pumilio "Black Jeans"

Vivi Hops

Vivi Hops is a world renowned frog breeder. She specializes in the preservation of endangered frog species. Vivi is an invaluable resource within the frogger community. She is coming to Frog Con to educate the masses on the importance of buying captive bred frogs!

Favorite Frog: Golden Mantella

Robbie Ribbit

Robbie Ribbit was raised by a feral group of frogs. Deep in the forests of Colombia, Robbie learned the ancient spiritual secrets of the frogs. We hope he shows up.

Favorite Frog: Variabilis Southern

Karl Punu

Karl Punu is a heavy hitter in the sector of phylobates. He has extensive experience dealing with these critters. He is a successful breeder and will be bringing dozens of terribilis froglets to sell after his keynote talk.

Favorite Frog: Phylobates teribilis

Sally "The Plug" Turner

Sally "The Plug" Turner earned her notoriety while running the infamous hralequin dart frog smuggling cartel. Now reformed, Sally joins us via Skype call to talk about her dark past within the illegal frog trade.

Favorite Frog: Harlequin Dart Frog
